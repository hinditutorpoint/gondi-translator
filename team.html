Rajesh Kumar Dhuriya

Founder & Lead Developer

About

Rajesh Kumar Dhuriya is a passionate software developer and cultural activist dedicated to preserving indigenous languages through technology. As the founder of Gondi Darshan, he has been instrumental in creating digital tools that make the Masaram Gondi script accessible to millions.

Born in Siddharth Nagar, Uttar Pradesh, Rajesh grew up witnessing the gradual erosion of tribal languages in his community. This inspired him to combine his technical expertise with his passion for linguistic preservation, leading to the creation of the Gondi Darshan platform.

With over 5 years of experience in full-stack development and a deep understanding of computational linguistics, Rajesh has successfully built transliteration engines, mobile applications, and educational resources that serve the Gond tribal community worldwide.
