FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I install the Gondi keyboard?
You can download the APK for Android or Windows installer from our Download page. Detailed installation instructions are provided with each download.
Is this tool completely free?
Yes! All our tools are 100% free and open source. You can use them without any limitations. We rely on donations to maintain and improve the project.
Can I contribute to the project?
Absolutely! We welcome contributions. Visit our GitHub repository to submit issues, pull requests, or suggestions.
Which platforms are supported?
Our web tool works on any device with a modern browser. We also offer native apps for Android and Windows. iOS support is planned for the future.
How can I learn to type in Gondi?
Check out our character reference table and example phrases. We also have video tutorials on our YouTube channel.
Do you support other tribal languages?
Yes! Visit our Projects page to see all tribal languages we support, including Kurukh, Santali, Ho, and more.
