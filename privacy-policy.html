Your Privacy is Our Priority! At Gondi Darshan, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect and use data. We collect minimal data and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gondi Darshan ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website gondidarshan.org, use our mobile applications, or interact with any of our services (collectively, the "Services").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy applies to all users of our Services, including visitors, registered users, and contributors.

Our Commitment: We follow privacy-by-design principles, collect only necessary data, use industry-standard security measures, and give you control over your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Create an Account: Name, email address, username, and password
  • Contact Us: Name, email, message content, and any attachments
  • Subscribe to Newsletter: Email address and communication preferences
  • Make a Donation: Payment information (processed securely by third-party payment providers)
  • Upload Content: Artwork, translations, comments, and other user-generated content
  • Participate in Surveys: Responses and feedback

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect:

Data Type What We Collect Purpose
Device Information Device type, operating system, browser type Optimize experience for your device
Usage Data Pages visited, time spent, features used Improve our services
IP Address Your internet protocol address Security and approximate location
Referral Data How you found our website Understand traffic sources
Cookies Session and preference data Remember your preferences

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Social Media: If you connect via social media platforms
  • Payment Providers: Transaction confirmation (not full payment details)
  • Analytics Services: Aggregated usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Services

  • Operating and maintaining our website and applications
  • Processing your requests and transactions
  • Enabling transliteration and translation features
  • Providing access to digital library and gallery content
  • Managing your account and preferences

3.2 Communication

  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Sending newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Notifying you about changes to our Services
  • Sending important administrative messages

3.3 Improvement & Analytics

  • Understanding how users interact with our Services
  • Identifying trends and usage patterns
  • Improving user experience and interface design
  • Developing new features and tools
  • Troubleshooting technical issues

3.4 Security & Legal

  • Protecting against unauthorized access and abuse
  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

5. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information! Your data is not for sale, and we will never share it with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

5.1 When We May Share Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental request
  • Protection: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Consent: With your explicit consent
  • Business Transfer: In connection with any merger or acquisition (with notice to you)

5.2 Public Content

Content you share publicly on our platform (such as artwork, comments, or translations) may be visible to other users and the public. Please be mindful of what you share.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure Storage: Protected servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and software updates
  • Monitoring: Regular security assessments and monitoring

No Absolute Security: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations).

Right to Object

Object to certain processing of your personal information.

Right to Portability

Request transfer of your data to another service provider.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for processing based on consent at any time.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hinditutorpoint@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Account Settings

You can manage your preferences through:

  • Account settings (if registered)
  • Unsubscribe links in emails
  • Browser cookie settings
  • Contacting us directly

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our Services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

For Parents: If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

Minors (13-18 years)

If you are between 13 and 18 years old, please review this Privacy Policy with your parent or guardian before using our Services. We encourage parents to monitor their children's internet usage.

10. International Data Transfers

Our Services are primarily operated from India. If you access our Services from outside India, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India or other countries where our service providers operate.

By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your information to India and other jurisdictions as described in this Privacy Policy.

Safeguards

When transferring data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards to ensure your information remains protected according to this Privacy Policy.

11.2 Third-Party Responsibility

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before interacting with them.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in our practices or Services
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Improvements in privacy protection
  • Feedback from users

Notification of Changes
