Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To preserve, promote, and digitalize the Gond tribal language, culture, and heritage through innovative technology and community-driven initiatives.

Gondi Darshan is a grassroots initiative dedicated to preserving the rich linguistic and cultural heritage of the Gond tribe, one of India's largest indigenous communities. With over 3 million speakers across central India, the Gondi language represents centuries of wisdom, tradition, and unique worldviews.

Our platform provides free, accessible tools for typing, transliterating, and learning the Masaram Gondi script, ensuring that this ancient writing system thrives in the digital age. We believe technology can be a powerful force for cultural preservation and community empowerment.

Language Preservation

Developing digital tools to keep the Gondi language alive and accessible to new generations.

Education & Resources

Creating learning materials, dictionaries, and educational content for Gondi learners.

Cultural Heritage

Documenting and showcasing Gond art, folklore, music, and traditional practices.

Community Building

Connecting Gond communities worldwide and fostering pride in tribal identity.

What We Do

Digital Tools & Technology

  • Masaram Gondi Transliterator: Free online tool for typing in Gondi script
  • Mobile Keyboards: Android and iOS keyboards for seamless Gondi typing
  • Windows Application: Desktop software for Gondi language support
  • Web-based Translator: Translate between Gondi, Hindi, and English

Educational Resources

  • Digital Library: Collection of Gondi literature, books, and manuscripts
  • Learning Platform: Interactive lessons for learning Masaram Gondi script
  • Character Reference: Comprehensive guides and documentation
  • Cultural Archive: Photos, videos, and recordings of Gond traditions

Community Initiatives

  • Art Gallery: Showcasing traditional Gond paintings and artwork
  • Folklore Collection: Preserving oral traditions, myths, and stories
  • Blog & Articles: Sharing knowledge about Gond culture and language
  • Support Programs: Direct support to Gond artists and content creators

Our Journey

2020
Project Inception

Gondi Darshan started as a passion project to create digital tools for the Gondi language.

2021
First Transliterator Launch

Released the first web-based Masaram Gondi transliterator with ITRANS support.

2022
Mobile App Development

Launched Android keyboard and mobile applications for wider accessibility.

2023
Community Growth

Reached 10,000+ downloads and built a vibrant community of Gondi language enthusiasts.

2024
Comprehensive Platform

Expanded to include gallery, library, folklore, and educational resources.
