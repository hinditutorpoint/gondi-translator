Featured Collection: Sacred Gond Art

Traditional paintings depicting creation myths and nature spirits

Gond art is characterized by intricate patterns of dots and dashes, creating elaborate designs that tell stories of gods, animals, and the natural world. Each piece is a visual narrative passed down through generations, where every dot and line carries meaning.
